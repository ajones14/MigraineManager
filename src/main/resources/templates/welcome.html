Welcome to Migraine Manager! We are here to assist you with tracking and managing your personalized triggers and symptoms so that you can take control of your migraines and your life.

Weather information including temperature, humidity, and barometric pressure enables you to observe how weather might be affecting you.
The personalized trigger and symptom lists enable you to track the information that is most important to you.
Our intuitive charts allow you to easily track which triggers and symptoms occur most often, and display monthly information on your migraine episodes.
